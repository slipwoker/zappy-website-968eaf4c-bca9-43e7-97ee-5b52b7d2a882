Contact.

We'd love to hear from you about upcoming pre-orders, studio pickup fairs, or any questions about our handmade ceramic pieces. Each inquiry helps us understand what resonates with collectors and how we can serve you best.

Whether you're curious about a specific piece, interested in future collections, or simply want to learn more about our process, reaching out is the first step toward finding a piece that speaks to you.

What to Include

Piece Interest: Let us know which collections or styles catch your eye—functional ware, sculptural pieces, or specific glazes.

Timeline: Share your ideal timeframe. Pre-orders typically take 4-6 weeks, and we announce pickup fairs monthly.

Pickup Availability: Mention your preferred pickup location or fair dates to help us coordinate.

Response Window: We typically respond within 2-3 business days. During busy studio periods (kiln firings, fair prep), it may take up to 5 days.

Pickup Fairs (How It Feels)

Each pickup fair is a calm, personal exchange—a moment to see your finished piece for the first time and connect with the artist who made it. There's a quiet anticipation in the air as collectors arrive, often discovering that the piece they ordered has evolved beautifully during the firing process.

These gatherings are intentionally unhurried. You'll have time to ask questions, learn about the glaze choices, and understand the care your piece needs. It's less transactional, more like visiting a friend's studio—a space where craft and conversation naturally intertwine.

Location

Pickup fairs take place at Tali's studio in a quiet neighborhood. The exact address is shared via email after you place your pre-order, ensuring a personal and secure handoff experience.

Pickup Day Flow

Fairs run in relaxed 2-hour windows, typically on weekend afternoons. You'll receive a suggested time slot to avoid crowding, though drop-ins within the window are always welcome. Plan for 10–15 minutes to collect your piece and chat.

Packaging & Care Handoff

Each piece is wrapped carefully in tissue and placed in a reusable cloth bag. Tali provides a brief care card explaining how to clean and handle your ceramic, plus tips for displaying or using it daily. Bring a tote if you're collecting multiple items.
